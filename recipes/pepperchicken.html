Pepper Chicken

Description

One of the Tasty and spicy Chicken dish.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Onion & Tomatoes
  • Cumin, Pepper & Coriander Powder

Steps

  1. In a large pan, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion, garlic, and ginger. Saut√© until the onion is translucent.
  2. Add the chopped tomatoes to the pan and cook until they have broken down and the mixture is saucy.
  3. Add the coarsely ground black peppercorns, cumin powder, coriander powder, turmeric, and salt to taste. Stir until the spices are fragrant.
  4. Add the chicken pieces to the pan and stir until they are coated in the spice mixture.
  5. Cover the pan and cook the chicken over medium-low heat for 15-20 minutes or until it is cooked through, stirring occasionally.
  6. Garnish the pepper chicken with chopped cilantro and serve hot with rice or naan bread.
