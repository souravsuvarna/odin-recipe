Biriyani

Description

Biriyani is the one of the famous south indian dish.


Ingrediants

  • Rice
  • Chicken
  • Vegitables

Steps

  1. Soak the rice in cold water for 30 minutes. Drain the water and rinse the rice until the water runs clear. Set aside.
  2. In a large pot, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and saut√© until it becomes translucent.
  3. Add the meat and cook until it is browned on all sides. Add the biryani spice mix, turmeric, and salt to taste. Stir until the meat is coated in the spices.
  4. Add the rice to the pot and stir until it is well combined with the meat and spices. Add the chicken broth and bring to a boil. Reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and let the biryani simmer for 20-25 minutes or until the rice is cooked through.
  5. Once the rice is cooked, turn off the heat and let the biryani rest for 10 minutes before serving. Garnish with raisins, almonds, or cashews, if desired.
