Butter Chicken

Description

Must Try chicken dish.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Butter
  • Cumin, Pepper & Coriander Powder
  • Vegitables

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the yogurt and lemon juice. Add the chicken pieces and stir until they are coated in the yogurt mixture. Cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, or up to 24 hours.
  2. In a large pan, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion, garlic, and ginger. Saut√© until the onion is translucent.
  3. Add the chopped tomatoes to the pan and cook until they have broken down and the mixture is saucy.
  4. Add the butter, tomato paste, garam masala, paprika, turmeric, salt, cumin, coriander, cinnamon, and cayenne pepper (if using). Stir until the spices are fragrant and the butter is melted.
  5. Add the marinated chicken pieces to the pan and stir until they are coated in the spice mixture. Cover the pan and cook the chicken over medium-low heat for 15-20 minutes or until it is cooked through, stirring occasionally.
  6. Add the heavy cream to the pan and stir until it is well combined with the chicken and spice mixture. Cook for an additional 5-10 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened.
  7. Garnish the butter chicken with chopped cilantro and serve hot with rice or naan bread.
